DynTech LLC is a company that manufacturers unique space suits for Mars colonizers!

The company was founded by Mr. Melon with the express intent of ferrying humans from Earth to Mars. To make that happen, extensive research has gone into atomic particle splitters and recomposers.

It was late 1920s when space travel was first envisioned by the Melon family. At the time, Sr. Melon did not have enough resources to invest in building such a machine. So he reached out to the secret travellers society.
